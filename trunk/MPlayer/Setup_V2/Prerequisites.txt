-------------
Prerequisites
-------------

The following tools are needed to compile the installer:

(1) Nullsoft Scriptable Install System (Unicode Edition) v2.46.5
    http://www.scratchpaper.com/
    http://code.google.com/p/unsis/downloads/list

(2) StdUtils plug-in for NSIS
    http://nsis.sourceforge.net/StdUtils_plug-in

(3) CPUFeatures plug-in for NSIS
    http://nsis.sourceforge.net/CPUFeatures_plug-in

(4) Aero plug-in for NSIS
    http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Aero_plug-in

(5) AccessControl plug-in for NSIS
    http://nsis.sourceforge.net/AccessControl_plug-in

(6) NewAdvSplash plug-in for NSIS
    http://nsis.sourceforge.net/NewAdvSplash_plug-in


The following software will be bundled in the installer:

(1) MPlayer on Windows, by Gianluigi Tiesi
    http://oss.netfarm.it/mplayer-win32.php

(2) SMPlayer front-end, by RVM
    http://sourceforge.net/projects/smplayer

(3) MPUI front-end, by Martin Fiedler
    http://sourceforge.net/projects/mpui/

(4) Binary Codecs, by The MPlayer Team
    http://www.mplayerhq.hu/MPlayer/releases/codecs/
